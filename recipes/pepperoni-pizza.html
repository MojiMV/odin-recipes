Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

Description

Indulge in the timeless delight of homemade pepperoni pizza, where simplicity meets sensational flavor. Our pepperoni pizza recipe is a true classic, featuring a perfectly thin and crispy crust that's the ideal canvas for a symphony of flavors. A generous layer of rich tomato sauce, sprinkled with aromatic oregano, forms the base, followed by a lavish scattering of gooey, golden mozzarella cheese. But the star of the show, the pepperoni slices, take center stage as they curl and crisp in the oven, releasing their savory, slightly spicy essence. Each bite is a harmonious blend of tangy, cheesy, and peppery goodness. Whether shared with friends during a game night or savored solo, this pepperoni pizza is the epitome of comfort food, bringing joy and satisfaction with every bite. So, fire up your oven, and embark on a culinary journey that celebrates the art of pizza-making.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Olive oil (for brushing the crust, optional)
  • Dried oregano (optional, for seasoning)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for added heat)
  • Salt and black pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven:
    Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can go, typically around 475-500°F (245-260°C). Place a pizza stone or baking sheet inside while preheating.

  2. Prepare the Dough:
    Roll out your pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness. You can make it round or rectangular, depending on your preference.

  3. Assemble the Pizza:
    Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza peel or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. If using a pizza stone, sprinkle cornmeal or flour on the pizza peel to prevent sticking.

  4. Add Sauce:
    Spread a generous amount of tomato pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.

  5. Layer Cheese:
    Sprinkle a generous layer of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.

  6. Add Pepperoni:
    Place pepperoni slices evenly over the cheese. You can use as much or as little as you like, depending on your preference.

  7. Season:
    Optionally, sprinkle some dried oregano and red pepper flakes over the top for extra flavor.

  8. Bake:
    Carefully slide the pizza (on parchment paper if using a baking sheet) onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven. Bake for about 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

  9. Cool and Slice:
    Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Then, slice it into wedges or squares, and serve hot.

Enjoy your homemade pepperoni pizza!

Lasagna Recipe

Description

Indulge in the comforting embrace of homemade lasagna, a timeless Italian classic that tantalizes taste buds with layers of rich flavors and creamy textures. Our lasagna recipe is a harmonious blend of tender lasagna noodles, velvety ricotta cheese, savory ground meat sauce, and gooey mozzarella, all bathed in a luscious tomato sauce. Each bite offers a symphony of tastes, from the subtle herbaceous notes of basil and oregano to the satisfying meatiness of the sauce. Whether you're serving it for a family dinner or a special occasion, this lasagna is a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone craving seconds. It's a labor of love that's worth every moment spent in the kitchen, resulting in a piping hot dish of pure comfort and culinary delight. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to savor the ultimate lasagna experience.

Ingredients


  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1-1/4 pounds bulk Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cans (one 28 ounces, one 15 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2/3 cup water

Steps


  1. Prepare the Ingredients:
    Gather lasagna noodles, ground meat (beef, pork, or a combination), ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, tomato sauce, onions, garlic, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper.

  2. Cook the Noodles:
    Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions until they are al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. Brown the Meat:
    In a large skillet, cook the ground meat over medium-high heat until it's browned and crumbled. Drain any excess fat.

  4. Sauté Onions and Garlic:
    In the same skillet, sauté chopped onions and minced garlic until they are soft and fragrant.

  5. Season the Sauce:
    Add tomato sauce, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet with the meat, onions, and garlic. Simmer for 10-15 minutes to let the flavors meld.

  6. Preheat the Oven:
    Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  7. Layer the Lasagna:
    In a baking dish, start with a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of cooked noodles, a layer of ricotta cheese, and a layer of mozzarella cheese. Repeat this process until you've used up all the ingredients, finishing with a layer of mozzarella cheese on top.

  8. Bake:
    Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the top is bubbly and golden

  9. Let it Rest:
    Allow the lasagna to rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing. This helps it set and makes it easier to serve.

  10. Serve and Enjoy:
    Cut into portions, serve your homemade lasagna hot, and savor the layers of deliciousness!

Enjoy your homemade lasagna!

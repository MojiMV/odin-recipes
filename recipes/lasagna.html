Lasagna Recipe

Description

Indulge in the comforting embrace of homemade lasagna, a timeless Italian classic that tantalizes taste buds with layers of rich flavors and creamy textures. Our lasagna recipe is a harmonious blend of tender lasagna noodles, velvety ricotta cheese, savory ground meat sauce, and gooey mozzarella, all bathed in a luscious tomato sauce. Each bite offers a symphony of tastes, from the subtle herbaceous notes of basil and oregano to the satisfying meatiness of the sauce. Whether you're serving it for a family dinner or a special occasion, this lasagna is a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone craving seconds. It's a labor of love that's worth every moment spent in the kitchen, resulting in a piping hot dish of pure comfort and culinary delight. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to savor the ultimate lasagna experience.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1-1/4 pounds bulk Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cans (one 28 ounces, one 15 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2/3 cup water
